Data Deletion Request

In compliance with Google Play policy, we provide this page for users to request the deletion of their game data and accounts, even if the app is no longer installed.

How to request deletion

To delete your Constella account and all associated data (including your level creations, high scores, and game progress), please follow these steps:

  1. Identify your Player ID (found in the game's Settings menu).
  2. Send an email to suhasdgd@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  3. Include your Player ID in the body of the email.

Once received, we will verify the request and permanently delete all data associated with your ID from our servers (Firebase) within 7 business days.

What data is deleted?

Upon processing your request, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • Your unique Firebase Anonymous/Google UID.
  • All puzzles you have published to the community.
  • Your likes, play history, and progress records.

Note: Data stored locally on your device will remain until you uninstall the app or clear its cache.

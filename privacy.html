Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information that can identify you. However, our third-party partners (Firebase and AdMob) may collect technical data such as:

  • Device information (Model, OS version).
  • Advertising ID (for personalized ads, if consented).
  • Crash logs (via Firebase Crashlytics) to help us fix bugs.
  • Gameplay progress (stored locally or via Firebase Analytics).

2. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use your Advertising ID to serve relevant ads. You can opt-out of personalized advertising through your Android device settings.

3. Data Deletion

Most game data is stored locally on your device. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the application or clearing the app cache in your device settings.

To request deletion of data stored on our servers (such as community levels or progress sync), please visit our Data Deletion Request Page.
